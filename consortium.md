# Project Consortium

The BUGATTI project brings together leading research institutions and industry partners to advance practical threat modeling and security guidance.

---

## Research Partners

### KU Leuven - DistriNet (Project Lead)

<div class="partner-block">
  <p><strong>Department:</strong> Computer Science, Faculty of Engineering Science</p>
  <p><strong>Research Group:</strong> DistriNet - Distributed Systems and Computer Networks</p>
  
  <p>DistriNet is a research group at KU Leuven focusing on distributed systems, software engineering, and security. With decades of experience in security research, DistriNet conducts cutting-edge research on:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>Application security and web security</li>
    <li>Privacy-enhancing technologies</li>
    <li>Secure software engineering</li>
    <li>Mobile and IoT security</li>
  </ul>

## Industry Advisory Board

The BUGATTI project is guided by an advisory board of industry partners who provide practical insights, real-world use cases, and validation of research outcomes. This ensures that project deliverables are immediately applicable in industry settings.

### Advisory Board Members

<div class="advisory-section">
  <p><em>Our advisory board consists of leading companies and organizations in various sectors, including:</em></p>
  
  <ul class="board-list">
    <li><strong>Technology sector</strong> - Software development and IT service companies</li>
    <li><strong>Financial services</strong> - Banks and fintech organizations</li>
    <li><strong>Healthcare</strong> - Medical device and healthcare IT companies</li>
    <li><strong>Manufacturing</strong> - Industrial and IoT-focused companies</li>
    <li><strong>Government & Public sector</strong> - Public administration and security agencies</li>
  </ul>

## Collaboration Opportunities

### For Research Institutions

We welcome collaboration with other research groups working on:
- Threat modeling methodologies
- Security engineering
- Privacy and data protection
- Applied cryptography
- Secure systems design

### For Industry Partners

Companies interested in:
- Applying advanced threat modeling in their development processes
- Contributing use cases and real-world scenarios
- Accessing cutting-edge security research
- Participating in workshops and training
